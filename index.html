Jujutsu Kaisen

Bestfriend/s of MC (Yuji Itadori)

Megumi Fushiguro

Nobara and Yuji fought well together near the end of Jujutsu Kaisen's first season, and she and Yuji enjoy teasing Megumi together with their endless antics. Only true friends get along like this.

Nobara Kugisaki

Nobara treats Yuji as one of her closest friends and confidante, going so far as to mourn his “death,” even though she'd only known him for only a few days up until that point.

Satotu Gojo

Satoru and Yuji trust each other completely, and Satoru has saved Yuji's life more than once against a variety of villainous curses. His teaching methods are unconventional, but they work, and Yuji is always ready to learn

Aoi Todo

Todo is a student at Kyoto High and meets Yuji at the Kyoto Goodwill Event. Todo immediately befriends Yuji when he learns they have the same taste in women, even going so far as to teach him how to use Black Flash.
